the prophecy

"There I was, just standing there, when what I wanted to do was forbidden. We had been taught from a young age that the Sacred Text was untouchable. How a single touch would cause the skin from our fingers to singe and crisp, falling off our bones before our very eyes. The long hair that resembled blackened waves would wither down to the scalp. If one dared to even speak a syllable from the text, their own tongue would absorb the pages until it choked them."
